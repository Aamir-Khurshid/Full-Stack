About New Delhi

New Delhi, the capital of India, is a city where history and modern life exist side by side. From centuries-old monuments to wide avenues and vibrant markets, the city reflects the rich cultural heritage of the nation.

It is home to iconic landmarks like India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhavan, and Connaught Place, while also serving as a major center for politics, culture, education, and business. New Delhi welcomes people from all parts of the country, creating a unique blend of traditions, languages, and lifestyles.

Whether you are exploring historical sites, enjoying street food, or experiencing the fast-paced urban life, New Delhi offers something for everyone.
